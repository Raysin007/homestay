Welcome to Summit Himalayan

Discover the perfect blend of comfort and authentic Himalayan hospitality at our family-run homestay in Darjeeling.

Your Home in the Himalayas

Established in 2010, Summit Himalayan Homestay has been welcoming travelers from around the world to experience the magic of Darjeeling. Our family-run establishment offers a warm, personalized experience that you won't find in large hotels.

Nestled in the picturesque hills of Darjeeling, our homestay provides stunning views of the Himalayan range while being conveniently located near major attractions.
