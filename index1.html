Places Near Us

Explore these wonderful attractions just a short distance from our homestay

Ghoom Railway Station

Visit the highest railway station in India, part of the UNESCO World Heritage Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. Experience the charm of the historic toy train.

2.5 km away

Peace Pagoda

This beautiful Buddhist stupa promotes world peace and offers panoramic views of Darjeeling and the Himalayan range. A perfect spot for meditation.

4 km away

Ghoom Monastery

Also known as Yiga Choeling Monastery, this is one of the oldest Tibetan Buddhist monasteries in Darjeeling, housing a 15-foot statue of Maitreya Buddha.

1.8 km away

Dali Monastery

The Druk Thupten Sangag Choling Monastery is a beautiful Tibetan Buddhist monastery known for its colorful frescoes and peaceful atmosphere.

5 km away

Tiger Hill

Famous for its spectacular sunrise views over Kangchenjunga and other Himalayan peaks. A must-visit for breathtaking panoramic views.

6 km away

Batasia Loop

A spiral railway loop where the toy train makes a 360-degree turn, offering magnificent views of Darjeeling town and the mountains.

3 km away
